The Beginning

Born from a Memory and a simple Dream.

As a child, I was always excited about Diwali and fireworks. Like every kid, I eagerly waited for the festival season, imagining the joy of lighting crackers and celebrating with friends and family.

Firecrackers were not just products for me — they were moments of happiness, laughter, and celebration.

A Silence in the Celebration.

However, due to financial challenges during my childhood, buying crackers was not always possible.

Many times, I had to watch others enjoy the celebration while quietly wishing I could experience the same excitement.

"Those moments left a deep impression on me. They taught me how meaningful even a small spark of celebration can be in a child’s life."

🚀

The Mission

A Mission to bring Joy.

Years later, when the opportunity came to build a business in Sivakasi — the heart of India’s fireworks industry, I saw it as more than just a business opportunity.

It became a mission to bring joy to more families and children. That is why Auraa Crackers focuses strongly on affordability and customer happiness.

Every child deserves the joy.

The goal was never just to sell fireworks, but to make celebrations more accessible so that more families can enjoy the festive spirit without financial burden.

For us, money is only a byproduct. The true success of Auraa Crackers lies in the smiles of children, the excitement of families, and the memories created during festivals.

Today, Auraa Crackers stands on a simple belief: Every child deserves the joy of celebration.
